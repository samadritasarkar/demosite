Home

Aminia is located in one of the busiest places in Kolkata-Garia. It is a one-stop destination for people who are in love with North Indian and Chinese cuisine. At Aminia, all your cravings for the specific authentic dishes, get an answer. To go with the flavors and ingredients, the restaurant sets an equally subtle ambiance that caters to the taste of people from different backgrounds. The considerate staff gives quick services. It is an ideal place for people who have an inclination towards non-vegetarian dishes. The nearest landmark is State Bank of India Branch.

About Us

Aminia is a branded chain of restaurants with twelve locations in Kolkata, India, which specialises in traditional Mughlai cuisine of Eastern India. It was founded by Mrs.Puja Bagchi in 1999. The restaurant is most noted for its special Biryani and Aminia Special. It was opened in 1929, and is a growing chain of restaurants within West Bengal. The restaurant is one of the fastest growing restaurants in Eastern India. It has been consistently ranked in the top 3 Mughlai Restaurants in Kolkata. The first outlet was opened in Zakaria Street opposite the Nakhoda Masjid.
